Sales Performance Dashboard

Dynamic revenue analytics tracking customer behavior, market performance, and product trends.

Executive Overview

This interactive dashboard provides real-time visibility into sales performance across multiple dimensions: customer segmentation, geographic markets, product portfolio, and temporal trends. The analysis identifies high-value customers, revenue concentration by market, and year-over-year growth patterns to support strategic planning and resource allocation.

Interactive Sales Dashboard

Key Metrics & Insights

The dashboard tracks critical sales KPIs across four strategic dimensions:

  • Customer Performance: Top 5 customers by revenue contribution, enabling targeted account management and retention strategies.
  • Geographic Distribution: Revenue and sales quantity breakdown by market, revealing regional growth opportunities and saturation points.
  • Product Portfolio: Top 5 products by revenue, informing inventory planning and promotional focus.
  • Temporal Trends: Year-over-year revenue patterns identifying seasonal fluctuations and growth trajectories.

Strategic Applications

This dashboard enables data-driven decision-making across multiple business functions:

  • Sales Strategy: Prioritize high-value customer relationships and identify cross-selling opportunities.
  • Market Expansion: Evaluate underperforming markets for targeted growth initiatives.
  • Inventory Management: Align stock levels with top-performing product demand.
  • Forecasting: Leverage historical trends for accurate revenue projections.

Tools & Methodology

Platform: Tableau Public
Techniques: Dynamic filtering, calculated fields, time-series analysis, ranking functions, geographic mapping
Features: Interactive filters allow users to drill down by time period, market, or product category
Data Refresh: Dashboard designed for automated updates with new sales data
