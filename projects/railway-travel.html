Railway Travel Company Analysis

Strategic insights on passenger travel patterns, country performance, and GDP correlation with business success.

Executive Overview

This analysis explores railway passenger data across multiple countries to identify performance trends, revenue opportunities, and correlations between economic indicators and business metrics. The project combines data storytelling with an interactive dashboard for strategic decision-making.

Interactive Dashboard

Data Story: 5-Part Analysis

Key Findings

  • Travel Patterns: Ten countries are responsible for carying over 91% of the distance traveled by passengers worlwide.
  • Economic Correlation: Higher GDP per capita correlates with Easy of doing business score
  • Strategic Recommendation: France emerges as a top pick, thanks to passenger volume with robust economy and bussiness-friendly environment.

Tools & Approach

Platform: Tableau Public
Techniques: Data storytelling, calculated fields, geographic analysis, correlation analysis
Data Source: https://www.openrailwaymap.org/, World Bank DataBank
