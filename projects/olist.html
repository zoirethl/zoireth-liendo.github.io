Olist E-commerce Strategy & Operations Analysis

Identifying growth opportunities and logistics bottlenecks in Brazil's largest marketplace.

Project Overview

This analysis explores the Olist dataset to understand sales evolution, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency. The goal is to provide data-driven recommendations to increase revenue and operational consistency[cite: 5, 6].

Executive Summary & Key Findings

  • Sales Seasonality: Strong performance between May and August, followed by a sharp decline in September. Revenue is driven more by volume fluctuations than price changes[cite: 5].
  • Customer Retention: Recurring customers represent a smaller segment but spend significantly more than new ones. High-impact opportunity in retention strategies.
  • Product Dynamics: A small group of categories dominates total sales. Some high-volume categories have low margins, suggesting a need for pricing optimization.
  • Logistics Risk: Late deliveries correlate with higher shipping costs, indicating specific localized bottlenecks.

Business Recommendations

1. Retention over Acquisition

Invest in loyalty programs targeting recurring customers to boost LTV (Lifetime Value).

2. Operational Efficiency

Investigate the specific logistics routes contributing to delays to reduce shipping overhead.

3. Portfolio Diversification

Reduce dependency on top-tier categories to mitigate risk across the marketplace.
